Destinations & Activities

New York

Description :

New York is so big and so diverse a place that you could live here four lifetimes and not experience all the amazing things the city has to offer. Even figuring out where to start your NYC trip is a daunting task. You have a variety of activities to choose from such as celebrating New Years on Time Square, visiting 9/11 Memorial, trying new foods from different quisines and much more!

Dubai

Description :

New Year's Eve in Dubai - a mixbag of fireworks, feasting and festivities. Ring in the new year on a kayak, sandboard down a dune, or spend your day in the clouds - whatever it is you decide to do, from luxury dinner in cruise to helicopter ride,Bujr Khalifa biggest fireworks and many more!

Paris

Description :

Paris for the New Year, the city of light offers plenty of stimulating and festive ways to celebrate. In fact, the capital of France is one of the most colorful and exciting places to celebrate this holiday, whether you prefer clubbing the night away, a traditional French meal with a view, or a simple glass of champagne shared among a small group of loved ones. Or a a luxury dinner in Eiffel Tower with your loved one in the most romantic city!

Rome

Description :

New Year’s Eve is a street festival in the Roman city center. Many Romans eat at home or in the restaurant and usually at midnight they flock to the city center, to the Colosseum or to the lookout hills to celebrate and watch the fireworks. In normal years the city organizes a 24-hour festival around the Circus Maximus!

Rio De Janeiro

Description :

Rio de Janeiro has always been considered one of the best New Year’s Eve destinations in the world. Besides it being the middle of summer and Rio’s incredible beaches, the main attraction is the festive Cariocas (people from Rio). They are warm and friendly and all about a great party. Rio New Year is the place to be and they’ll make sure you feel welcome and have the time of your life!
